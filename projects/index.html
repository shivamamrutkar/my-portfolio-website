SecureSwap: Rebuilding Trust in Peer-to-Peer Commerce

In today's digital marketplace, the fear of scams prevents countless valuable transactions between strangers. SecureSwap was conceptualized to solve this problem by creating a "digital handshake." It's a platform designed to act as a neutral third party, holding funds in secure escrow until both the buyer and seller are satisfied. The vision is to foster a safe and reliable environment where anyone can confidently exchange goods and services, unlocking the true potential of the P2P economy.

Skills: Web Development, Market Research, Payment Gateway Integration
